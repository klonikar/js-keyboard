Multi lingual virtual keyboard. You can type in any language of your choice. You might always have wanted to type in your mothertongue without having to spell it out in english. Why take crutches of english when you want to communicate in your mothertongue. Transliteration should always be the last resort when communicating in your mothertongue. It is only a workaround due to a lack of keyboards in your language.
